About Me

I’m Dani, the founder of Individuality—a North Carolina-based clothing brand built to inspire confidence, authenticity, and self-expression through fashion. This brand is rooted in my own journey—balancing motherhood, growth, and ambition while learning the power of embracing who I am. I created Individuality to offer more than just clothing. I wanted to send a message: that strength starts with knowing yourself and showing up as that person every day.
